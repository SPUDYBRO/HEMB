IT Support Technician

JOB TYPE: Full Time

SALARY: $60,000 - $90,000

SUPERVISOR: IT Support Manager

POSITION DESCRIPTION REFERENCE NUMBER: IT30


As an IT Support Technician, you will provide IT support to a broad range of companies.

    Responsibilities:

  • Determining suitiable software and hardware solutions for clients.
  • Installing software to common operating systems.
  • Designing web sites using HTML5.
  • Advising clients on steps to take when encountering software or computer issues.
  • Identifing and responding to software and computer issues.

    Essential Qualifications:

  1. Completed a suitable tertiary course in Information Technology.
  2. An indepth understanding of HTML5.
  3. Knowledge in Troubleshooting.
  4. Understanding of Network Infrastructure.
  5. Knowledge of Computer Hardware.
  6. Proficiency in Operating Systems.
  7. Knowledge of Security Practices.
  8. Familiarity with Database Concepts.

    Preferable Qualifications:

  1. Prior work experience in an IT support position.
  2. Prior work experience in customer support.
  3. Work well unsupervised.
  4. Passion in Information Technology.

    Benefits

  • 20 days of Paid-Time-Off per year
  • Dental insurance
